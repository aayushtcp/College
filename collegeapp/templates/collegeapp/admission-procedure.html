Admission Procedure

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and IT (BSc CSIT)

Candidates must have completed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. A minimum grade of C in each subject is required. A-Level students must have a minimum grade of D in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

Applicants should have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board or institution. A minimum of 45% (second division) or a C grade in all subjects is required.

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Applicants should have secured at least a D+ grade in all subjects in their 10+2 or equivalent studies. Admission will be granted based on an interview with eligible candidates.
