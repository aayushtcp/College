About +2 and Syllabus

The +2 program is an intermediate education program that prepares students for further studies in undergraduate courses. It focuses on providing foundational knowledge in various streams like Science, Commerce, and Arts.
The program typically lasts for two years and is essential for students who wish to pursue higher education in various disciplines, including engineering, medicine, business, and humanities.

Mission of +2 Program

  1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of core subjects in the chosen stream.
  2. To prepare students for higher education in diverse fields of study.
  3. To foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills among students.

Scope of +2 Program

  1. +2 graduates have opportunities to pursue undergraduate programs in various fields, such as engineering, medicine, business, and more.
  2. Graduates can also opt for vocational courses or enter the job market after completing the program.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Completion of secondary education (10th grade) or equivalent.
  2. Minimum required marks for admission (varies by institution).
